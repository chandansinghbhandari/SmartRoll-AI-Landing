## 🌟 Overview

SmartRoll AI is an advanced attendance management platform that leverages Artificial Intelligence, Facial Recognition, Voice Biometrics, and QR-based enrollment to automate attendance tracking while preventing fraudulent attendance practices.

This repository contains the official landing page designed to present the SmartRoll AI ecosystem, product features, technology stack, workflow, and platform benefits.
